Design Pattern: Few Hues, Many Values


What?

This pattern is created by the choice of one or two colors, then creating values of those colors to use throughout your website.


When?

Best used for creating a harmonious look to your website, without adding too many colors or clashing colors.


Why?

Using values of hues rather than many hues, allows you to avoid making your website distracting or overly contrasted. It’s also a great technique for adding dimension to your pages.


How?

Choose the hues you want to use, the fewer is better. Then vary the brightness of each or even lessen saturation to create small offsets of those hues. Basically, it’s like choosing paints for a palette. Then apply those to the website in effective ways to create dimension and character.


I created this page and used the color picker along with the Minty bootstrap from Bootswatch theme to choose colors that varied in value and added to elements on this page.
