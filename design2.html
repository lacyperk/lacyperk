Design Pattern: Sitemap Footer


What?

Footer at the bottom of web pages that serve as a global navigation to pages.


When?

Your site has numerous pages that have simple and few categories.


Why?

Allows users to view more navigation options, thus making it simpler to jump from main pages to subpages.


How?

Designate a footer for the webpage, then add your main categories and sections for your site. Below place any important subpages of each category.
