Design Pattern: Social Links


What?

These are links to take users to your social media accounts, typically on the home page.


When?

Use if you want users to realize the presence you carry online on media accounts, keeping in mind that the user may then leave your website. This is important to keep purposeful and could help with gaining more followers on your accounts especially if you post continuously, for example a blog.


Why?

This is helpful if you are trying to gain more visitors to your social media channels. It also potentially could do the reverse and get people from media to go to your website. It’s useful for users looking for you social media online presence.


How?

Create a small section or set aside an area on your homepage for links to your social media, typically brand icons are used for this as they are much more noticeable on the page. It’s important to remember to either set this to open in a new window or tab or make it a button that simply does an action such as “following” on Facebook. This prevents the user from completely leaving your website.
